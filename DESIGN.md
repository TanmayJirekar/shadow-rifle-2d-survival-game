## Shadow Rifle: 2D Survival Ops

**Concept:** Dark military side-scrolling arena shooter. Fight waves of enemies, earn coins, unlock weapons, conquer levels.

**Core Mechanic:** Wave-based combat — survive escalating enemy waves per level. Aim with mouse, shoot on click/space. Enemies melee-attack when in range.

**Art Style:** Dark military neon-green aesthetic. Canvas-rendered entities with AI-generated character sprites.
